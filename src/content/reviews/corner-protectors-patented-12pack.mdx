Most corner protectors are flat L-shaped pieces that only fit perfect ninety-degree corners. The soft, pliable material cushions impact from any direction. ### Pre-Applied Adhesive

Each protector comes with adhesive already applied — peel the backing and stick. No separate adhesive tape, no double-sided sticky pads, no extra steps. One second per corner. Walk into a rental, identify sharp edges, peel and stick twelve protectors, done. The entire room is baby-proofed in under three minutes.

## What We Don't Love

**They do not stick well to textured surfaces.** A rough-hewn wooden coffee table in one rental defeated the adhesive within an hour. The protector slid off and our daughter found it on the floor. On textured stone, porous wood, and unfinished surfaces, the adhesive simply does not have enough contact area to grip. For these surfaces, you need protectors with stronger industrial adhesive — but those will also leave residue.

**Determined toddlers can peel them off.** Our daughter, at 22 months, figured out that the protectors were separate objects attached to the furniture. She began picking at edges with her fingernails. On smaller corners where less adhesive surface was in contact, she succeeded in removing them. We repositioned them and pressed firmly, which bought us another day. By age two, she could remove any protector she could reach if she was motivated. The protectors are more effective for crawlers and early walkers than for older, more dexterous toddlers.

**You need to clean the surface first for best results.** The adhesive works best on clean, dry surfaces. A dusty table edge, a surface with cooking oil residue, or a corner with furniture polish will reduce adhesion significantly. We now carry a small pack of alcohol wipes in the same bag as the protectors — a quick wipe before application doubles the hold time. This extra step is minor but necessary for reliable performance.

**Twelve is enough for one room, not an entire house.** A multi-room vacation rental with furniture in the living room, dining room, kitchen, and bedrooms can easily have twenty or more sharp corners. Twelve protectors cover the highest-priority areas but not every edge. We carry two packs when traveling to larger rentals, bringing our total cost to $18 for full coverage.
